Tea

Tea is a classic Indian drink made out of an infusion of tea leaves, milk, spices and sugar



Ingredients

  • Tea Leaves
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Powedered Cardamon
  • Ginger


Steps

  1. Boil the water
  2. Add tea leaves
  3. Add grated ginger
  4. Add sugar
  5. Add milk and bring to a boil
  6. Simmer for 5 minutes
  7. Serve Hot
